PaliGemma - Multimodal Vision-Language Model

Ever wondered how your phone can recognize both your face and your voice? PaliGemma is my take on that magic—it's a Transformer model that can see and read at the same time! I built it by mixing CLIP, SigLip, and some clever tricks like KV-Caching and Top-P Sampling. Imagine an app that can look at a photo of a Mumbai street and instantly describe the scene in Hindi or English—auto-rickshaws, pani puri stalls, and all. That's the kind of real-world fun this project aims for!
